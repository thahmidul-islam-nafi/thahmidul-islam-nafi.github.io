Welcome to My Profile

Hello! I'm Thahmidul Islam Nafi, an ASIC Physical Design Engineer at PrimeSilicon Technology (BD) Ltd. specializing in ASIC Physical Design and Optimization.
Besides ASIC Design, I also have experience working with Deep Learning, Transformer Architecture, Wireless Sensor Networks, and Web Development.

My Domains of interest are VLSI, Electronics, Deep Learning, Computer Vision and Embedded Systems.
